About Us

We are a leading supplier of Cold Rolled Steel and other Steel products in Coimbatore region. We are in this field for the past 50 years. We have roots linked to the people who started steel industry in Coimbatore region. We are catering to more than 5000 customers in Tamil Nadu and Kerala, who have given us the requirement directly. We have signed MoU with SAIL and other such agreements with other manufactures like Tata, Essar, Jindal etc which helps us to bring quality steel with competitive price to Coimbatore region.
